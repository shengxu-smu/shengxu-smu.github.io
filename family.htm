Introduction

I was born in a love family. My parents only received a couple of years' education because of poverty, but they teach their children with love. They work hard and use their own hands to support their children to live better. I am their youngest child. I have two brothers and two sisters. They all have established their own families. Here is a photo of us.

I got married on July 13, 2001. My wife's name is Mei Mei. (In Chinese, the first 'Mei' means plum blossom and the second one means rose.) She is the one who is worth my forever cherishing and loving and who can give me a full and happy life.

A Poem I Wrote for My Wife 

  It is you!




Who changes the distance of my space?
It is you!
Who changes the pace of my time?
It is you!

Who makes my night sweet?
It is you!
Who makes my day shining?
it is you!

It is you
Who send me wishes with snowflakes in winters.
It is you
Who bring me happiness with songs in my trips.

It is you
Who make my heart beat for you.
It is you 
Who feel the resonance of my soul with you.

You are roses emitting fragrance in sunshine.
You are plum blossoms giving colors in snow.

You are love!
You are power!
You are my life!
